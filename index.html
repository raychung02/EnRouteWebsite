About

En Route is an urban dance showcase hosted by Haluan, a competitive hip-hop dance team housed under the Filipino Student Association at the University of California, Santa Cruz. En Route was founded by the 2014-2015 Haluan directors Adlina Basuki, Ray Chung, and Alexis Roney in hopes that it would help connect Santa Cruz to the larger dance community in California.

En Route was first held on May 23, 2015 at the Cabrillo College Crocker Theater and was the first urban dance showcase to take place in the Santa Cruz area. The showcase featured teams from all across California such as Sacramento, Los Angeles, San Diego, and many more. Following the showcases success, Haluan is now planning to host En Route annually.
